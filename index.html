Empowering Youth Health Awareness

The Red Ribbon is a digital health platform designed to provide comprehensive, accessible, and accurate HIV education and resources for adolescents between the ages of 15 -19. It serves as a safe, inclusive space where teenagers can access information on HIV topics such as HIV Basics & Awareness, HIV Prevention for Adolescents, HIV & Relationships, Mental Health & Stigma, HIV & Future Goals.
