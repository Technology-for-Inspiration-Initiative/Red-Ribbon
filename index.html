The Red Ribbon Platform

Red Ribbon platform is designed to provide comprehensive, accessible, and accurate sexual health education and resources for adolescents.

It serves as a safe, inclusive space where teenagers can access information on topics such as sexual and reproductive health, consent, contraception, sexually transmitted infections (STIs), mental health, and healthy relationships.
