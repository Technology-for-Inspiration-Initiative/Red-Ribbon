The Red Ribbon is a digital health platform designed to provide comprehensive, accessible, and accurate sexual health education and resources for adolescents. It serves as a safe, inclusive space where teenagers can access information on topics such as sexual and reproductive health, consent, contraception, sexually transmitted infections (STIs), mental health, and healthy relationships. The platform combines educational tools, expert advice, interactive forums, and multimedia resources, empowering young people to make informed decisions about their sexual health and well-being. Through a digital-first approach, it fosters engagement, reduces stigma, and ensures that sexual health information is easily accessible and relatable to teenagers in a confidential and supportive environment.
